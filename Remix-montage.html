Remix Montage

In this piece, I wanted to perceive that spreading around misinformation— or not giving enough information— could cause the community to become a little more toxic. A lot of people tend to not really put much research on certain topics, they’ll just believe what the media tells them and not bother checking for any proof. News outlets even hold back some information just so they could put someone in a bad light, which could lead to mobbing, and some fights breaking out. Only a few people look through the sources and research to try and assist the situation, But nowadays it has become harder and harder to believe what’s true or false.
